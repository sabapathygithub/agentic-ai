Here’s a beginner workout plan you can follow for 4 days a week at the gym. This workout plan focuses on different muscle groups each day, allowing for balanced training and sufficient rest.

### Beginner Workout Plan (4 Days a Week)

#### Day 1: Upper Body (Push)
- **Warm-up (5-10 minutes)**: Light cardio (jogging, cycling, or jumping jacks)
- **Workout**:
  1. Bench Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Overhead Press (Dumbbell) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Incline Dumbbell Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Lateral Raises - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  5. Tricep Dips (Bodyweight or Assisted) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes)**: Stretching focusing on shoulders, chest, and triceps.

#### Day 2: Lower Body
- **Warm-up (5-10 minutes)**: Light cardio (elliptical or cycling)
- **Workout**:
  1. Squats (Bodyweight or Barbell) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Lunges (Bodyweight) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
  3. Leg Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Calf Raises - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  5. Glute Bridges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes)**: Stretching focusing on quads, hamstrings, and calves.

#### Day 3: Upper Body (Pull)
- **Warm-up (5-10 minutes)**: Light cardio (rowing machine or jumping rope)
- **Workout**:
  1. Pull-Ups (Assisted if necessary) - 3 sets of 5-8 reps
  2. Bent Over Rows (Dumbbell or Barbell) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Seated Cable Rows - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Face Pulls (Cable Machine) - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  5. Bicep Curls (Dumbbell) - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes)**: Stretching focusing on back and biceps.

#### Day 4: Full Body/Core
- **Warm-up (5-10 minutes)**: Light cardio (jumping jacks or dynamic stretches)
- **Workout**:
  1. Deadlifts (Dumbbell or Barbell) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Plank - 3 sets holding for 20-30 seconds
  3. Mountain Climbers - 3 sets of 15 reps per leg
  4. Kettlebell Swings - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  5. Russian Twists (with weight) - 3 sets of 10-15 twists per side
- **Cool Down (5-10 minutes)**: Stretching focusing on the entire body.

### General Tips:
- **Rest**: Ensure to rest around 1-2 minutes between each set.
- **Progression**: Focus on form before increasing weights. Gradually increase the weight or reps as you get stronger.
- **Hydration**: Stay hydrated before, during, and after your workout.
- **Nutrition**: Consider a well-balanced diet to support your workout routine.
