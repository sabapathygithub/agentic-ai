# Healthy Lifestyle Routine

## Daily Schedule

- **7:30 AM**: Wake up  
- **7:30 AM - 8:00 AM**: Morning Routine  
  - Hydrate (Drink a glass of water)  
  - Quick meditation or stretching  

- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM**: Breakfast  
  - **Paleo Diet Recipe**: Paleo Egg Scramble  
    - **Ingredients**: 2 eggs, cooked chicken or beef, spinach, tomatoes, and herbs  
    - **Instructions**:  
      1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add olive oil.  
      2. Saut√© the spinach and tomatoes.  
      3. Add the cooked chicken/beef.  
      4. In a bowl, whisk eggs and pour them over the mixture, cooking until scrambled.  

- **9:00 AM - 10:30 AM**: Gym/Strength Training  
  - Minimalist Exercises:  
    - **Pull-Up**  
    - **Goblet Squat**  
    - **Push-Up**  
    - **Standing Lunge**  
    - **Single-Leg Deadlift**  

- **10:30 AM - 11:00 AM**: Post-Workout Snack  
  - Paleo Option: Chicken or Beef + Vegetables  
  - **Supplements**: Omega-3 (after meals containing fat for optimal absorption)  

- **11:00 AM - 1:00 PM**: Work/Study  

- **1:00 PM - 2:00 PM**: Lunch  
  - **Paleo Diet**: Mutton or Chicken with Assorted Vegetables  
  - **Supplements**: Vitamin D3 + K2 (with a meal including fat)  

- **2:00 PM - 4:00 PM**: Work/Study  

- **4:00 PM - 4:30 PM**: Snack/Break  
  - Paleo Option: Handful of nuts or sliced vegetables  
  - **Supplements**: Magnesium  

- **4:30 PM - 6:00 PM**: Work/Study  

- **6:00 PM - 7:00 PM**: Evening Gym Session (Optional)  
  - Focus on cardio or flexibility exercises  
  - **Supplements**: Creatine (post-workout for muscle recovery)  

- **7:00 PM - 8:00 PM**: Dinner  
  - Paleo Diet: Beef or Chicken + Varied Vegetables  
  - **Supplements**: Collagen (post-meal)  

- **8:00 PM - 10:30 PM**: Leisure Time  
  - Read, spend time with family, or engage in hobbies  

- **10:30 PM**: Start Wind Down  
  - Limit screen time, dim lights, and practice relaxation techniques  

- **11:00 PM**: Sleep  
  - Aim for 8 hours of sleep  

## Supplements Information  
- **Omega-3**: Take with lunch or dinner.  
- **Vitamin D3 + K2**: Take with breakfast or lunch containing fat.  
- **Creatine**: Take post-workout for muscle recovery.  
- **Magnesium**: Take during the afternoon snack.  
- **Collagen**: Take post-dinner.  

## Note  
- Stay hydrated throughout the day  
- Adjust meal portions based on your energy needs and goals  
- Consult with a healthcare provider regarding supplements.
