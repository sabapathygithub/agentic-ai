# Weekly South Indian Paleo-Friendly Recipes

This document contains a week's worth of South Indian recipes that are compliant with both paleo and low carb high fat diets. Each recipe includes ingredients, preparation time, cooking time, and instructions.

## Day 1: Coconut Chutney with Vegetable Sticks

### Ingredients:
- 1 cup grated coconut
- 2 green chilies (adjust to taste)
- 1/4 cup roasted chana dal
- 1/2 inch ginger
- Salt to taste
- Water as needed
- Assorted vegetable sticks (cucumber, carrot, bell peppers)

### Preparation Time: 10 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 0 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Blend grated coconut, green chilies, roasted chana dal, ginger, and salt with water to form a smooth paste.
2. Serve with assorted vegetable sticks as a refreshing snack.

## Day 2: Egg Curry (Masala Muttai)

### Ingredients:
- 4 eggs, boiled and peeled
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 green chilies, slit
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

### Preparation Time: 10 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 20 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cook for a minute.
3. Stir in chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili, cumin powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
4. Gently add boiled eggs and coat them in the masala. Cook for 5 minutes.
5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot.

## Day 3: Palak Paneer (Spinach with Cottage Cheese)

### Ingredients:
- 200g spinach, blanched
- 100g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 2 green chilies
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 tsp garam masala
- Salt to taste

### Preparation Time: 15 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 15 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Blend blanched spinach with green chilies to make a smooth paste.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté onions until translucent.
3. Stir in chopped tomatoes, cook until mushy.
4. Add spinach paste, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
5. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5 minutes. Serve warm.

## Day 4: Zucchini Roti with Coconut Oil

### Ingredients:
- 1 large zucchini, grated
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp almond flour
- Salt to taste
- Coconut oil for cooking

### Preparation Time: 10 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 15 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Mix grated zucchini, egg, almond flour, and salt to form a dough.
2. Heat coconut oil in a skillet. Form small rounds from the dough and cook until golden brown on both sides.
3. Serve with coconut chutney or yogurt.

## Day 5: Grilled Fish with Spices

### Ingredients:
- 2 fillets of fish (salmon or tilapia)
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander for garnish

### Preparation Time: 10 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 10 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Marinate fish fillets with turmeric, red chili powder, lemon juice, and salt for 30 minutes.
2. Grill on medium heat for 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
3. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

## Day 6: Cauliflower Rice and Mixed Veggies

### Ingredients:
- 1 medium cauliflower, grated or riced
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 green chilies
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- Salt to taste
- Coconut oil for cooking

### Preparation Time: 10 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 15 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Heat coconut oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté onions until golden.
2. Add mixed veggies and green chilies, cook for 5 minutes.
3. Stir in grated cauliflower and salt, cook for another 5-7 minutes. Serve warm.

---

## Day 7: Baked Eggplant with Spiced Yogurt

### Ingredients:
- 1 large eggplant, sliced
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- Salt to taste
- Olive oil for brushing

### Preparation Time: 15 minutes  
### Cooking Time: 30 minutes  

### Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F). Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and place on a baking sheet.
2. Bake for 20-25 minutes until tender.
3. Mix yogurt with cumin, red chili powder, and salt. Serve baked eggplant with spiced yogurt.
