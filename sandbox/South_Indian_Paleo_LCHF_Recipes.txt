# South Indian Paleo and LCHF Recipes

## Key Characteristics of Paleo & LCHF Diets
- Focus on whole, unprocessed foods.
- High in healthy fats, moderate in protein, and low in carbohydrates.
- Excludes grains, legumes, dairy, refined sugar, and processed oils.

## Recipe 1: Cauliflower Rice Upma
### Ingredients:
- 2 cups cauliflower rice
- 1/2 cup chopped onions
- 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1/4 cup green peas (optional)
- 2-3 green chilies, chopped
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

### Instructions:
1. Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add mustard seeds and urad dal; sauté until the dal is golden.
3. Add onions, green chilies, and tomatoes; cook until soft.
4. Stir in cauliflower rice and peas; cook for 5-7 minutes.
5. Season with salt, garnish with coriander, and serve hot.

## Recipe 2: Coconut Flour Dosa
### Ingredients:
- 1 cup coconut flour
- 1/4 cup water (add more if needed)
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp turmeric (optional)
- Coconut oil for cooking

### Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix coconut flour, water, eggs, salt, and turmeric.
2. Heat a non-stick pan and add coconut oil.
3. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the pan and spread it out.
4. Cook until edges lift; flip and cook until golden.
5. Serve with coconut chutney or sambar.

## Recipe 3: Egg Curry (Kodi Kura)
### Ingredients:
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1/2 cup chopped onions
- 2-3 green chilies, slit
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- Fresh coriander for garnishing

### Instructions:
1. Heat coconut oil in a pan; sauté onions until translucent.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and green chilies; cook till tomatoes soften.
3. Stir in red chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala; mix well.
4. Add boiled eggs (cut in halves) and coat them in the mixture; cook for 5 minutes.
5. Garnish with coriander and serve with cauliflower rice.

## Recipe 4: Avocado Raita
### Ingredients:
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1 cup coconut yogurt (or unsweetened yogurt if not strictly paleo)
- 1/2 tsp cumin powder
- Salt to taste
- Coriander leaves for garnish

### Instructions:
1. Peel and mash the avocado in a bowl.
2. Mix in coconut yogurt, cumin powder, and salt.
3. Garnish with coriander leaves; serve chilled.

## Conclusion
These recipes are not only low in carbohydrates but also rich in flavors and nutrients. Enjoy your South Indian Paleo and LCHF journey!
